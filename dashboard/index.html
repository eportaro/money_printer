        How to read this dashboard

        Operational definitions

        Prediction is what the model thinks will win.

        Market price is Polymarket's implied probability.

        Edge is model probability minus market price.

        Signal is the simulated trade selected by the strategy.

        Alignment shows whether the signal follows or goes against the model prediction.

        Trade result shows whether the simulated signal made money.

        High prediction accuracy does not always mean profitable trades.

        Bucket warning: a bucket can have high accuracy but poor trading if the strategy buys contrarian longshots or enters too late.
